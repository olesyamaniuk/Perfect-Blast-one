How to Play

  • Match Cubes

    Tap to match two or more cubes of the same color and clear the board.

  • Use Boosters

    Unlock powerful tools like Lightning, Magic Wand, Broom, and Dice to tackle tricky puzzles.

  • Complete Objectives

    Solve diverse challenges such as rescuing owls, gathering diamonds, and clearing webs.

  • Team Up

    Join teams, participate in tournaments, and earn extra lives by helping your teammates.

  • Daily Events

    Take part in unique events like the Embers Chase to win exclusive prizes.

FAQ

  • How do I start playing? 
    Download the game from Google Play and complete the tutorial to begin.
  • Are there in-app purchases?
    Yes, optional purchases are available for boosters and additional lives.
  • Can I play offline?
    Yes, Perfect Blast supports offline play for uninterrupted fun.
  • What are the team features?
    Join teams to chat with teammates, compete in tournaments, and earn collective rewards.
  • How do power-ups work?
    Match multiple cubes to activate power-ups like Lightning and Magic Wand for explosive effects.
  • How many levels are there?
    The game offers over 2100 levels with more added regularly.
  • What is the Embers Chase event?
    Blast yellow cubes during this limited- time event to win grand prizes.
  • Who is the developer?
    Perfect Blast is developed by Outlane, Inc., known for creating engaging and visually appealing games.
